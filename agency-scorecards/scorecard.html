Comments

The U.S. Department of Commerce (DOC) is honored to receive an “A+” on our FY 2020 Small Business Procurement Scorecard. We are appreciative to the U.S. Small Business Administration for the public recognition of our efforts to create contract and subcontract opportunities for small business concerns in all categories. Our FY 2020 achievement is especially remarkable because of the added challenge of working during the COVID-19 pandemic. Still, it is with great pride that we celebrate our accomplishment. DOC's sustained record of outstanding achievement is a reflection of the hard work and commitment of our workforce to the Small Business Program. Among the fourteen federal agencies with at least $5 billion in eligible contract dollars, DOC is one of four agencies with the highest share of prime contracting dollars awarded to small businesses at 44% and we achieved the highest share of prime contracting dollars awarded to WOSB at 12%. DOC understands and appreciates the important role of small businesses in supporting our economy and job creation. Therefore, in FY 2021 and beyond, we will continue to strengthen our Small Business Program and identify new contract opportunities to help small and disadvantaged businesses grow and succeed.
